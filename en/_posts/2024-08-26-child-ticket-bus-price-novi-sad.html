Question

How much does a bus ticket cost for a 7-year-old child in Novi Sad (for example, route 7a) if the ticket is purchased on the bus?

Please refer to any published document that describes these rules.

Answer

According to the current Price List for single tickets purchased on board vehicles in city, suburban, and intercity transport, as well as the price of a daily ticket in city transport, as of 05.02.2024, the price of a single ticket purchased on board for children aged 6 to 10 in the first zone is 38 dinars.
