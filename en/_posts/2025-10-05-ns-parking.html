Dear ...,

If you have paid for parking in the red zone and your SMS message is still valid, and during this time you moved to the blue zone, you do not need to pay for the blue zone. This is because the red zone has a higher priority, and during inspection, your vehicle will be registered as having paid for parking.

However, if you paid for parking in the blue zone and then moved to a zone with a higher priority (red or extra), the system will not recognize your payment, as you paid for a lower priority zone but are located in a higher priority one. In this case, you need to pay for the zone you are actually in.
